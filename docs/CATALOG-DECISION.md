# Arctic Mini4WD Initial Catalog Decision

## Scope

The initial public shopping experience is limited to the progression:

**First Car → Box Stock → First Upgrade → B-MAX**

Open Class/Open Tournament equipment is postponed.

## Catalog size

| Tier | Count | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Core Stock | 50 | Products worth keeping locally when demand supports it |
| Expansion Stock | 28 | Add after sales show which chassis and upgrades are popular |
| Special Order / Collector | 8 | Do not treat as normal local inventory |
| **Total** | **86** | **20 cars and 66 parts** |

## Recommended car platforms

The beginner shop should prioritize these chassis:

1. **VZ** — light, simple and easy to explain.
2. **FM-A** — popular front-motor choice and useful for technical tracks.
3. **AR** — broad parts ecosystem and suitable beginner/B-MAX base.
4. **MA** — easiest dual-shaft PRO platform for beginners.
5. **MS** — keep a smaller selection for customers specifically wanting MS.
6. **Super-II / Super X-family** — limited support rather than a major launch focus.

## Core product families

- Complete car kits
- Chassis-specific Basic Tune-Up Sets
- Tuned and Hyper-Dash motors
- Brake sponge and brake stays
- Standard/slim mass dampers
- 13mm front and 19mm rear roller choices
- Stabilizers
- FRP plates before expensive carbon plates
- Super-hard and low-friction tire choices
- Bearings, reinforced shafts and common gear sets
- Screws, spacers, lock nuts and essential maintenance tools

## Products intentionally not prioritized

- Open Class anchor/pivot systems
- Custom-cut plates
- Homemade damper mechanisms
- Large quantities of rare carbon colors
- Every cosmetic wheel/roller color
- Legacy chassis inventory without proven local demand
- High quantities of the fastest motors

## Manual work remaining for the owner

For each product selected for sale, enter or approve:

- purchase cost
- selling price
- actual stock
- preorder/special-order status
- an image owned by Arctic Mini4WD or supplied with commercial-use permission
- final publish approval

Products absent from the curated list can still be added manually through the normal admin product form.

## Import behavior

The initial catalog must import as drafts only:

- unpublished
- zero stock
- zero price
- no image
- coming soon

This prevents unfinished products from appearing publicly.
